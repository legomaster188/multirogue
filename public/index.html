☠ MULTIROGUE ☠

A shared, persistent ASCII dungeon. Move with WASD/arrows (and Y U B N for diagonals). Bump monsters to attack. The dungeon is dark — rooms light up as you enter them. Descend with >, climb with <. Mages/Rangers Fire at foes. Eat rations E, quaff potions Q, read scrolls (R map, T teleport). Revive a fallen ally by stepping into them. Carry the Amulet of Yendor from depth 8 back to the surface to win.
